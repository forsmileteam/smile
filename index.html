Tips for better Mental Health

During the pandemic situations, Here are the few tips from our experts for better Mental Health...

Tip 1 : Take a break from News

Information Overloaded can be upsetting. Make efforts to switch off screens and relax a while.

Tip 2 : Take care of your body

Meditate, eat well-balanced meals,sleep, exercise,take deep breaths. Aside from staying safe, stay healthy.

Tip 3 : Make time to unwind

Use few hours of your day to do something you enjoy.

Tip 4 : Always stay connected

It is normal to feel sad, stressed, confused, scared or angry during a crisis. Talking to people you trust can help. Contact your friends and family.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's Smile ?

Smiles is community start-up program, which helps conduct Psychology surveys to understand the Mentality of person using Psychology based trained machine learning algorithm.

Why Smile now ?

While the world is struggling to arrest the spread of a global pandemic, we can clearly see a major social and Psychological impact on the whole population casing Mental illness like Stress, Depression, Anxiety. Which might even ends up with Suicide. Hence it is the responsibility of organization to monitor the Mental health for the welfare of the employees and for better productivity.

How Smile Intelligent Algorithm trained ?

Smile's team have trained the Intelligence bot with sample survey conducted, user feedbacks, past pandemic research, Psychology based algorithm & insights.

How long does it take to complete Smile survey ?

Smile survey will be consisting 15-20 basic questions based on current trends, which hardly take 5minutes to complete. Once all the users completes the survey, it will take a minimum of 2-3 Days to build the organization level insight report.

Does Smile team provide Counselling ?

Yes, After the survey analysis, We find people with mental illness and provide them Medical counselling with the helps of Psychology Doctors. We keep these data confidential too.
